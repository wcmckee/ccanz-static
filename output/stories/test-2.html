Find Creative Commons Content

  • For general content, as well as links to a dozen search engines, visit Creative Commons International.
  • For music, visit Jamendo and CC Mixter.
  • For photos, visit Flickr Commons.
  • For general media, visit SpinXpress.
  • For general content and public domain works, visit Wikimedia Commons.
  • For New Zealand content, visit Digital NZ.
