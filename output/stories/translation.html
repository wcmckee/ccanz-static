Draft Translation of Creative Commons 4.0 Licences into Te Reo Māori

This page contains a draft translation of the Creative Commons 4.0 licences into te reo Māori. If you wish to provide any feedback on this translation, please send a message to the Public Lead of Creative Commons Aotearoa New Zealand Matt McGregor: matt@creativecommons.org.nz, or get in touch via Twitter @cc_aotearoa. The public comment period ends on 1 April, 2015.
