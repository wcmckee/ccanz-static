Our Roadmap

Posted: 2010-09-20 15:20 [Source]
Tags:  Creative Commons

How can we best provide promotion and education about Creative Commons to our community? We welcome you to visit our jurisdiction wiki page to see what we're already planning to do over the next six months. There are lots of ways you can give us ideas and feedback about what you'd like to see us achieve in the future, you can

  • email us at admin@creativecommons.org.nz
  • post a comment to our discussion list (an existing example is here)
  • add to some existing feedback on our forums
  • write something in the discussion section of our wiki page

Our helpful CCANZ Panel will be digesting your ideas at their next meeting in December, at which point we will extend the forecast accordingly. We're looking forward to hearing your thoughts!
