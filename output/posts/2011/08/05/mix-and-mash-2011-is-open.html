Mix and Mash 2011 is open

Posted: 2011-08-05 12:50   |  More posts about Mix and Mash

On 4 August, DigitalNZ launched Mix and Mash: the great NZ remix and mashup competition for 2011. There are juicy prizes, categories and content/data pools - not to mention the stellar judges. You have six weeks to create an award winning masterpiece...

Data Mashup Categories

  • Open Government Data Mashup
  • Environment Data Mashup
  • Sport and Recreation data mashup
  • Support for Canterbury Earthquake Recovery Data Mashup
  • Anything Goes Data Mashup

Creative Remix Categories

  • Infographics
  • Digital Storytelling
  • Literature Remix
  • Photo Remix
  • Anything Goes Creative Remix

Need more reasons to enter?

Lead judges are Nat Torkington and CC New Zealand's Advisory Panel Member Helen Baxter. Our favourite guru Lawrence Lessig will help select winners for the Supreme Award for Creative Remix (sponsored by CC New Zealand), and the Supreme and Outstanding Awards for Data Mashup.

The top winners will receive $10,000 and $5,000 awards, and there are lots of other exciting prizes (thanks to the sponsors).

Now get invloved! If you're not sure where to start, try the getting started guide. Schools, check out this page of resources especially for you.

Happy remixing.
